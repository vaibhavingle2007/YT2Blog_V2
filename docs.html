YT2Blog Pro Documentation

Learn how to convert YouTube videos into professional blog posts with our comprehensive guide.

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to convert your first YouTube video into a blog post:

  1. 1
    Copy the YouTube URL

    Find a YouTube video you want to convert and copy its URL from your browser.

  2. 2
    Paste the URL

    Paste the YouTube URL into the input field on the YT2Blog home page.

  3. 3
    Configure Options

    Select your preferred language, template, and enable AI features as needed.

  4. 4
    Generate & Edit

    Click "Generate Blog" and wait. Then edit and export your content!

How It Works

YT2Blog Pro uses advanced AI technology to transform video content into written form:

1

Transcript Extraction

We extract the video's transcript using YouTube's caption system or speech-to-text AI.

2

AI Processing

Our LLM analyzes the content, structures it, and applies the selected template and options.

3

Blog Generation

The AI creates a professionally formatted blog post ready for publishing.

Templates

Choose from 4 professionally designed templates:

Article

Standard blog post format with introduction, body, and conclusion. Best for general content.

Tutorial

Step-by-step format with numbered sections. Perfect for how-to videos and coding tutorials.

Review

Product/service review format with pros, cons, and ratings. Great for review videos.

Summary

Concise summary with key takeaways. Ideal for long videos or lectures.

AI Features

AI Humanization

Makes content sound natural and human-written by removing robotic patterns, adding conversational flow, and varying sentence structure.

Fact Cleanup Mode

Removes filler content, "subscribe and like" prompts, sponsor mentions, intro/outro fluff, and off-topic tangents. Keeps only valuable information.

Code Detection

Automatically detects programming content and formats it with proper code blocks, syntax highlighting, and technical explanations.

FAQ

What videos work best?

Videos with clear speech and captions work best. Tutorials, how-to videos, reviews, and educational content produce the best results.

Can I use the generated content commercially?

Yes! Content generated is yours to use. However, always respect the original video creator's rights and provide attribution where appropriate.

Why did my generation fail?

Common reasons: video has no captions, video is private/age-restricted, or video is too long (over 2 hours). Try a different video or contact support.

How accurate is the translation?

Our AI produces high-quality translations that read naturally in each language. However, technical terms and proper nouns may occasionally need manual review.
